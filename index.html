About Me

I’m a passionate Environment Designer and UI/UX Designer focused on creating immersive game worlds and user-friendly mobile app experiences. I enjoy designing detailed environments, engaging level layouts, and clean mobile interfaces that feel smooth, modern, and easy to use, with the goal of delivering creative and polished designs that leave a lasting impact.

With skills in UI/UX Design, Frontend Development, Environment Design, Graphic Design, and 3D Art, I combine creativity with technical knowledge to bring ideas to life using tools like Unreal Engine, Blender, Figma, and Photoshop. I’m especially passionate about game level design, where I create balanced, visually rich spaces that guide players naturally through exploration and challenges. I continuously improve my workflow, learn new techniques, and stay updated with modern design trends to grow as a designer. My dream is to become a Professional Designer and Top-Level Designer, contributing to meaningful projects and building interactive experiences that feel exciting, immersive, and memorable.
